## 1. Product Overview

### 1.1 Vision
**iLoveAudio** is an **offline-first audio utility app** that enables users to quickly process audio files directly on their device, inspired by the simplicity and effectiveness of iLovePDF.

The app focuses on **speed, privacy, simplicity, and reliability**, not professional audio production.

---

### 1.2 Problem Statement
Most existing audio editing apps:
- Are complex and difficult to use
- Require internet connectivity
- Depend on cloud processing
- Ask for user login or subscriptions upfront
- Compromise user privacy

Users need:
- Simple, fast audio tools
- Offline processing
- No login or setup
- Predictable and repeatable workflows

---

### 1.3 Solution
A **tool-based, offline audio utility app** where:
- Each tool does one job well
- All processing happens locally
- The UI is minimal and intuitive
- No account or internet is required

---

## 2. Target Users

### 2.1 Primary Users
- Students (lectures, voice notes)
- Professionals (meetings, recordings)
- Podcasters (basic trimming)
- Content creators (quick edits)
- General smartphone users

---

### 2.2 User Goals
- Trim audio quickly
- Merge multiple audio files
- Convert audio formats
- Reduce audio file size
- Extract audio from video
- Export files without quality loss
